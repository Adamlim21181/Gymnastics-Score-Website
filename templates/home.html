Instructions

Step 1

First, add some gymnasts. Navigate to the 'register gymnast' page and fill in the details to add a gymnast to the first box. After that, your gymnast should appear in the table below. If you need to make changes to your gymnast's information, you can do so using the form at the bottom of the page.

Step 2

Secondly, add some scores. Navigate to the 'Add scores' page, where you will find a table displaying your registered gymnasts. Below the table, you can input your gymnast's score. Once you do this, the gymnast's score should be added to the table underneath. If you need to edit the score, you can do so using the form at the bottom of the page.

Step 3

After completing steps 1 and 2, you can access the leaderboard. Visit the leaderboard page and select the level

Step 4

You can now add additional gymnasts and scores. If you wish, you can revisit and modify your gymnasts and scores.
