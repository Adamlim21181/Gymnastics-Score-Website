Gymnastics Scoring

Welcome to my Gymnast's competition score calculator. You can add gymnasts and scores to create a leaderboard for your competition. Here are some instructions to help you get started.

Step 1 - Adding Gymnasts

To add your gymnasts to the competition, go to the gymnast page enter your gymnast's name and level in the first form, and click submit. Your gymnast should be added to the table underneath the form!

Step 2 - Adding Scores

Next, to add your gymnast's scores, go to the scores page and enter your gymnast's ID (which can be found in the table above the form). Select an apparatus. Finally, add the execution scores and difficulty scores and click submit. Your scores should be added to the table underneath the form!

Step 3 - Leaderboard

Almost there! Go to the leaderboard page and select the level leaderboard you want to view. You will find links at the top of the page to navigate different apparatuses.

Step 4 - Editing and Deleting

Finally, you can edit and delete your gymnasts and scores! On the gymnast page and scores page, at the bottom, there will be two forms, one for editing and one for deleting. Just fill out these forms, and you should see the changes in the same table above!
