Instructions

Step 1

First, add some gymnasts. Go to the 'register gymnast' page and enter the details to add a gymnast. Your gymnast will then appear in the table below. To make changes to a gymnast's information, use the form at the bottom of the page.

Step 2

Next, add some scores. Go to the 'Add scores' page where you’ll see a table with your registered gymnasts. Enter the gymnast’s score below the table. The score will be added to the table. To edit a score, use the form at the bottom of the page.

Step 3

After adding gymnasts and scores, check out the leaderboard. Go to the leaderboard page and choose the level.

Step 4

You can now add more gymnasts and scores. If needed, you can also update the existing gymnasts and scores.
